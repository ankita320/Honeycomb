Ankita Saha

Hello! I am Ankita and I'm a rising 8th grader from NYC. I am a Kode with Klossy 2020 scholar. Some things to know about me are, I love bullet journaling, drawing, and some k-pop dancing. I love Kpop! Anyway, I started Kode with Klossy around two weeks ago, and somehow from that two week program- I'm here with my peers building a website. From the coding program I've learned so many things I never thought I'd be able to learn. I was able to learn javascript, making buttons do things, and so much more. I have found a new love for coding from this. There are so many programs like this where you have the opportunity to gain so much knowledge. However, sometimes, we just don't know about them. Through a quick google search, it may be challenging to find the coding program you prefer. Everything is so jumbled, with so much info all at once. The information isn't very narrowed therefore, making it difficult to find a coding program. Another thing is that- there's the sterotype with women. "Women in tech?" "Women can't be working in the stem field." Those are all stereotypes. None of those things are true. I believe that women have the ability to take on the STEM field and be amazing at it. In this website, you will find so many programs geared toward women. Also, you can actually find everything without anything being too crowded. Our purpose is to assist those trying to find the program for themselves. Buzz with impact. passion. empowerment.
